Introduction

Sound is a form of energy produced by vibrating bodies and transmitted as a wave through a medium (solid, liquid, or gas). It cannot travel through a vacuum because particles are needed to carry vibrations.

Nature of Sound

  • Sound is a mechanical wave (requires a medium).
  • It is a longitudinal wave, meaning particles vibrate parallel to the direction of wave motion.
  • Regions of compression (high pressure) and rarefaction (low pressure) are formed.
