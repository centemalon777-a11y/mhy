Definition: Measurement is the process of finding the size, length, or amount of something, typically using standard units.

Basic Quantities: Length (meter), Mass (kilogram), Time (second).

Derived Quantities: Area, Volume, Speed, Acceleration, etc.

Measuring instruments include rulers, stopwatches, micrometer screw gauges, and vernier calipers.
